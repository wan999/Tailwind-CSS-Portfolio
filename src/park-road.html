Park Road

Self-initiated project based on the history of the buildings around Park Road, an area of inner city Liverpool currently undergoing urban regeneration. Highlighting buildings of historical importance. I investigated the city's archives to find pictures of landmarks; past and present near the location to use within the book. The ticket on the cover is based on the ticket design of the Liverpool Overhead Railway, which used to be a dominant feature of the area. The railway was dismantled in 1957.
