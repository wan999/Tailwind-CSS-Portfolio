Makefest

Promotional material created for Liverpool Makefest 2015 - the first maker event in the north-west of England. Makerfairs are for tech companies and educators to network, exchange ideas and promote themselves to the general public. Print documents e.g. trifold leaflets, A3 posters, A5 flyers and bunting were created to advertise the event. The brand colours and styling were kept consistent across all print materials.
