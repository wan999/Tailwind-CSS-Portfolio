FACT Liverpool

I was invited to create print certificates which were designed for FACT, an art gallery based in Liverpool. Designed as a thank you from the organisation to the gallery assistant volunteers based on the hours they volunteered at the art gallery. Different concepts were created, first variations of the design as an informal document which could be posted, however the final design was chosen with a more formal and professional visual language.
